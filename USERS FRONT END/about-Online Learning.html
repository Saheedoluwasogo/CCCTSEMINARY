    Online Learning
    With CCCTSeminary

    57,000 Registered Online Students

    Welcome to the Online Learning Programme of CCCTS, where our mission to train disciples, scholars and ministers is extended beyond walls and borders. Rooted in our motto “The Word Cleanses the World” (John 15:3) and guided by the Holy Spirit, our digital platform offers you the flexibility to engage in sound theological education from wherever you are.

    Join Now
    Online Philosophy

    At the Celestial Church of Christ Theological Seminary (CCCTS), our online philosophy is built upon the belief that spiritual transformation knows no distance. Whether in a classroom or across continents, every lesson, prayer, and study session is an encounter with the living Word of God.

    Kingster's Principle

    The Kingster’s Principle is the foundation of our seminary’s philosophy of learning. It teaches that true success — in study, ministry, and life — begins when divine wisdom governs human understanding. Knowledge apart from the Spirit leads to pride, but knowledge under the Spirit leads to purpose.

    Key Of Success

    At the heart of every CCCTS program — on-campus or online — lies The Key of Success. This guiding principle declares that excellence in ministry and academics is achieved through faith in God, discipline of mind, obedience to truth, and diligence in service.

    Our Philosophy

    Our philosophy is rooted in the eternal message of John 15:3 — “Now ye are clean through the word which I have spoken unto you.” We exist to train ministers and scholars who live by that Word, teach it faithfully, and demonstrate its cleansing power in every sphere of life.

    Why Choose CCCT Theological Seminary?

    At CCCTS, you’ll learn to think biblically. Live faithfully. Lead graciously. And you’ll receive a seminary education that is both grounded in the unchanging truth of God’s Word and abundant in practical, integrated ministry expertise.

    Our Story

    It is a long established fact that, without God we cannot be here today.

    The Celestial Church of Christ Theological Seminary (CCCTS) was founded as a divine response to the growing need for sound, Spirit-led theological education within the Celestial Church of Christ Worldwide. Established along the sacred grounds of Km 53, Lagos–Ibadan Expressway, Ogun State, Nigeria, the Seminary stands as a beacon of light and learning—where the Holy Spirit meets scholarship to form leaders of faith and purpose.
